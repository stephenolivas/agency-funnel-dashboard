Definitions & methodology

Scope — Only the four agency-managed funnels (Instagram, X, Linkedin, Anthony X). No other funnel data is fetched or published.

Setter and Closer are two independent counts, not a split of one number. Setter is discovery calls held this period — leads that scored below the bar get vetted by a setter before a closer's calendar opens up. Closer is first sales calls. A lead with a discovery on the 19th and a sales call on the 22nd is counted once in each. The two columns do not add up.

Stuck — discovery calls that have produced no closer call at all. A discovery held in the last few days may simply not have converted yet, so a fresh week will always look worse than it is. Check the Lead Status column: Disqualified means it ended, Call Booked means it's still moving.

Show %, Qual %, CW % are measured against Closer, never Setter.  ·  Projected — end-of-month estimate at current daily booking pace: (Booked ÷ days elapsed) × days in month. Green = exceeding, yellow = on pace, red = behind. Funnels with no goal show —.

Cash Collected — entered manually in Close. A dash means not yet recorded, not zero. Cash may exceed gross when payments land from deals closed in a prior period.  ·  Email — partially masked.
