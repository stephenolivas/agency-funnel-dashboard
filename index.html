Scope — This dashboard covers only the five agency-managed funnels (Instagram, X, Linkedin, Anthony X, Anthony IG). No other funnel data is fetched or published.  ·  Setter / Closer — two independent counts, not a split. Setter is discovery calls held this period; leads that score below the bar are vetted by a setter before a closer's calendar opens up. Closer is first sales calls. A lead with a discovery on the 19th and a sales call on the 22nd is counted once in each — they are two separate calls, so the columns do not add up.  ·  Stuck — discovery calls that have produced no closer call at all. A discovery held in the last few days may simply not have converted yet. Re-running an archive refreshes this.  ·  Show %, Qual %, CW % are all measured against Closer, never Setter.  ·  Projected — End-of-month estimate based on current daily booking pace: (Booked ÷ Days Elapsed) × Days in Month. Green = exceeding pace · Yellow = on pace · Red = behind pace. Funnels without a goal show —.  ·  Cash Collected — entered manually in Close. A dash means not yet recorded, not zero. Cash may exceed gross when payments land from deals closed in a prior period.  ·  Email — partially masked.
